# About

Hi, I'm Caleb. I make crossword puzzles across different themes, sizes, and
difficulty levels, and publish them here so anyone can play them right in
the browser.

I started constructing crosswords in July 2026, after about a year of
becoming addicted to solving online and newspaper puzzles like the NYT
crossword. I'm no expert however, and US-specific clues especially, like
state capitals and baseball players, still trip me up. But solving enough
puzzles trains your pattern recognition and common crossword words do
start to surface on their own.

I built this site as a creative exercise, and to reward solvers for
thinking outside the box. For me, the real challenge is balancing
creativity with accuracy and elegance.

I've linked my blog below, where I write about other hobbies like music,
photography, and of course puzzle solving and making. I've also attached
my CV and a few other projects, in case you're curious. Thanks for reading
and visiting. Happy solving!
